Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Open the app and tap "Create Account" from the sign-in screen. You'll need to confirm your email address before you can sign in — check your inbox for a confirmation link after signing up.

I didn't receive my confirmation email.
Check your spam folder first. If it's still missing, open the app, go back to the "Check your email" screen, and tap "Resend Email." If you're still stuck, contact us at the email above.

How do I sync my data across devices?
Just sign in with the same account on each device — your prayer, fasting, Bible, and journal entries sync automatically.

How do I delete my account and data?
Open the app, go to the Home tab, and tap "Delete Account." This permanently removes your account and all associated data immediately. This cannot be undone.
